Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups ( 375 mL ) all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 1/2 tsp ( 7.5 mL ) baking powder
  • ½ tsp ( 2.5 mL ) salt
  • ½ cup ( 125 mL ) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup ( 250 mL ) granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature
  • ¾ cup ( 175 mL ) milk
  • ½ cup ( 125 mL ) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 4 cups ( 1 L ) icing sugar, sifted
  • 2 tsp ( 10 mL ) vanilla extract

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line 12 muffin cups with paper liners. Whisk together flour, baking powder and salt; set aside. In separate bowl and using electric mixer, beat butter with sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, incorporating each one fully before adding the next one; beat in vanilla.
  • Step 2: With mixer on low speed, add flour mixture in 3 parts alternately with milk in 2 parts, starting and ending with flour mixture, and scraping bowl as needed between additions.
  • Step 3: Spoon or scoop batter into prepared muffin cups, about two-thirds full. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes or until tester comes out clean when inserted into centre of cupcakes. Let cool completely on rack.
  • Step 4: Beat butter until light and fluffy. With mixer on low speed, beat in icing sugar, cream, vanilla and salt until smooth, adding up to 2 tbsp (30 mL) more cream if needed. Increase speed to high; beat for 1 to 2 minutes or until frosting is light and fluffy.
  • Step 5: Add frosting to piping bag fitted with round tip; pipe over cupcakes. Alternatively, spoon frosting into resealable bag and clip off one corner to pipe over cupcakes, or spoon onto cupcakes.
